A Technology-Driven Initiative to Improve Banking Services in Bangladesh

This website is a personal initiative aimed at making banking services in Bangladesh simpler, safer, and more technology-driven. It shares practical banking tools, guides, and real-life insights developed from professional experience to support customers, bank officers, and banking authorities.

Customers
Banking customers can broadly be divided into two categories: walk-in customers and account holders. Walk-in customers usually do not maintain an account and visit banks to deposit money or complete tasks on behalf of others. Many of them lack basic banking knowledge. For them, this website provides sample applications, guidance, and simplified instructions—especially for completing complex savings certificate purchase forms, which are very common in Bangladesh.

Account holders may be depositors or loan borrowers. For them, the site offers loan-related guidance, loan calculation tools, comparative views of different loan products, and repayment calculations—particularly for salaried personal loans from Agrani Bank PLC. Commercial loan concepts, eligibility criteria, and application guidance are also available in the Tools section. Read more

Bank Officers
Manual banking processes often consume a significant amount of working hours. To reduce this workload, the website shares productivity ideas and provides online banking tools. These include cross-matching systems for daily transfer registers and after-cover registers to detect manual alterations, online loan proposal preparation tools to replace handwritten proposals, and utilities to shorten long Statement of Affairs reports by removing unnecessary items.

An online mail merge system is also available, allowing officers to generate hundreds of official letters instantly using predefined formats. Read more

Authority / Admin
Banking executives often lose valuable time collecting manual reports from officers. This platform attempts to simplify reporting and information presentation so that management-level users can access structured and meaningful data more efficiently. Read more
