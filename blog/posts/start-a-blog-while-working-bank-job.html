How to Start a Blog While Working a Bank Job (Without Getting Overwhelmed)

By Abdul Latif · Educational content · Updated 2026

Choose a niche you can write forever

The easiest blog niche is the one you live every day. Banking + tech + productivity is perfect because you’ll always have new experiences.

Instead of writing about “everything,” pick a clear direction so readers know what to expect.

A simple content formula

  1. Problem (what people struggle with)
  2. Your experience (what you saw in real life)
  3. Steps (how to solve it)
  4. Mistakes (what to avoid)
  5. Checklist (quick summary)

SEO basics that actually matter

  • Write one clear main keyword per post (use it naturally in title and headings).
  • Use short paragraphs and helpful subheadings.
  • Add internal links to related posts.
  • Write a useful meta description (not spam).
  • Answer real questions (FAQ section helps).
AdSense note: Focus on helpful, original, non-copied writing. Avoid auto-generated spam content and excessive keyword stuffing.

Posting schedule you can maintain

  • Option A: 1 strong post per week (recommended).
  • Option B: 2 shorter posts per week if you have energy.
  • Batch writing: write on weekends, publish mid-week.

Conclusion

A blog grows with consistency, not perfection. If you publish helpful posts for 6 months, your website becomes an asset you own.
