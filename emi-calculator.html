EMI Calculator (Bangladesh)

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed monthly payment for a loan. This calculator helps you estimate your installment amount, total interest, and total repayment.

What is EMI and why is it important?
EMI means the fixed amount you pay every month to repay your loan. It includes both principal and interest portions. Knowing EMI in advance helps you plan your monthly budget and avoid over-borrowing.
How banks calculate EMI in Bangladesh
Most Bangladeshi banks use the standard reducing balance formula for EMI. EMI depends on loan amount, annual interest rate, and loan tenure. A higher interest rate or longer tenure increases total interest cost.
Loan tips before applying
Before taking a loan, ensure your EMI is affordable. A safe rule is to keep EMI within a reasonable portion of your monthly income (often 2/3 or 80% depends on bank policy). Always compare multiple bank offers and check hidden charges.
Disclaimer about results
This calculator provides an estimate only. Final EMI may vary due to processing fees, insurance, VAT, rounding rules, and bank-specific loan policy. Always confirm final repayment schedule with your bank.
