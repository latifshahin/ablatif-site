1) What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. They help websites remember preferences, measure performance, and improve user experience. Cookies do not usually contain personal identity information such as your name or password.

2) Why We Use Cookies

  • To understand how visitors use this Website (Analytics)
  • To improve performance and functionality
  • To measure and display advertising (AdSense)
  • To help protect the Website from spam and abuse

3) Google Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to measure website traffic and user behavior. Google Analytics may store cookies to collect anonymous data such as:

  • Pages you visit
  • Time spent on pages
  • Device and browser type
  • General location (city-level, not exact address)

4) Google AdSense Cookies

This Website may show advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to:

  • Display relevant ads based on browsing behavior
  • Prevent showing the same ad repeatedly
  • Measure ad performance and user interactions

Google and its partners may use personalized advertising cookies. You can control ad personalization using Google Ads Settings.

5) Managing Cookies

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect how certain tools and features work.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy
