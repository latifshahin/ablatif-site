1) What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your browser or device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information such as preferences, page settings, and browsing behavior.

2) Why We Use Cookies

Cookies help us:

  • Understand how people use our website (traffic analytics)
  • Improve tools, blog content, and website performance
  • Serve advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Measure ad performance and user engagement

Google may show personalized ads based on your browsing history. If you do not want personalized ads, you can turn off ad personalization using Google Ads Settings.

5) How to Control Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:
